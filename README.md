# path-to-the-hat

An interactive terminal game where players navigate through a field of holes to find their lost hat.

## How to Play

1. Use your keyboard to move:
   - W: Up
   - S: Down
   - A: Left
   - D: Right
2. Find your hat without falling into holes
3. Don't step outside the field!
